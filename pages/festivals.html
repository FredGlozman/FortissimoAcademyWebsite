FESTIVAL OPUS 21 2015


Click here to download our OPUS 2015 APPLICATION FORM in pdf


Dear Friends and Colleagues,

Fortissimo Academy of Music and Arts is announcing the Second edition of Festival OPUS 21 2015.

This Festival provides an unique opportunity for young classical musicians to perform in public and receive comments from renowned musicians and pedagogues from Montreal's CEGEPs,Schulich School at McGill University,UQAM,UDM,and Conservatoire de Montreal.

You are cordially invited to share this wonderful and exciting musical experience with us.

Festival will take place on the premises of the Academy Fortissimo located at 5555 Westminster Ave in Cote Saint-Luc,as well as on the premises of the UDM,UQAM and Conservatoire de Montreal,from April 25 to April 27,2015.

Fortissimo Academy is a young,dynamic,fast growing school of outstanding quality and care.We strongly encourage and support every endeavour,commitment and dedication along the artistic journey.

CATEGORIES AND REQUIREMENTS

CATEGORY "Enchanting Music" (competitive event)  Age:6-23

PIANO:
1.Two contrasting movements of Sonata or Sonatina,OR J.S.Bach's Prelude and Fugue from WTC
  2.One piece of choice.
TIME LIMIT: age 13 and under - 15 min
      age 14 and older - 20 min

STRINGS:
1. Two contrasting movements of Sonata or Sonatina,OR first movement of any Concerto,OR second and third movements of any Concerto
2.One piece of choice,OR one movement from J.S.Bach's Solo Sonatas and Partitas,OR slow movement of any Concerto
TIME LIMIT: age 13 and under - 15 min
        age 14 and older - 20 min

CATEGORY "Music for Me" (non competitive event) Age:4 to adult

PIANO,STRINGS: Two pieces of choice
TIME LIMIT:age 13 and under - 15 min
   age 14 and older - 20 min

Participants will perform in their appropriate age category defined as of January 1, 2015

Each age is a single category

Students may choose to participate in more than one instrument,as well as in both competitive and non-competitive categories
